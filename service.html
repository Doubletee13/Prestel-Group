Project Development and Deployment

Site identification and selection

Using our proprietary SWARM software, we quickly pinpoint the optimal mini-grid sites by remotely evaluating financial, technical, and geospatial data. Whether your electrification project targets a single village or a vast region, we deliver comprehensive site assessments ranked by profitability potential. When it's time for field verification and customer outreach, our software simplifies the process, enabling your team to operate swiftly and effectively.

Market Feasibility Study

Performing a thorough feasibility study before entering an energy access market is crucial to reducing investment risk. Our research team uses a proven framework to evaluate numerous market factors comprehensively. Field visits to the target region are often essential to supplement existing data with fresh primary research. Our analysts are skilled at identifying key indicators and understanding the unique characteristics of energy access markets. Along with delivering a detailed feasibility report, we provide strategic recommendations to help further mitigate risks.

Design Services

Off-grid energy systems come with unique demands compared to conventional grids. We create customized generation and distribution solutions tailored to diverse customer needs and regulatory standards. Our modular approach allows for easy expansion or integration with the main grid. Additionally, we offer turnkey electrical system templates designed for various mini-grid capacities.

Training

Establishing a long-lasting mini-grid business demands skilled operators who understand how to fully leverage our software and navigate the practical challenges of mini-grid operations. Equally important are field technicians capable of delivering reliable customer support and equipment upkeep. Our training programs equip all team members with the essential skills to manage and sustain successful mini-grid enterprises.

Customer acquisition

Even with a detailed feasibility study, there's no replacement for real-world market testing. In areas that differ from those already served by our systems, we highly advise launching a pilot project to verify market suitability and resolve technical or logistical issues before scaling up. Based on our experience, pilot projects help avoid costly mistakes and delays in the long run. We offer the required mini-grid infrastructure and handle the entire pilot process from setup to execution. During the pilot phase, we deliver thorough performance reports and strategic recommendations to refine deployment and maximize revenue.

Procurement and logistics

Off-grid energy projects require specialized components and streamlined coordination. We manage the sourcing of generation and distribution equipment to align with specific customer needs and regulatory standards. Our modular system designs support easy expansion or integration with the national grid when needed. Additionally, we provide ready-to-deploy electrical system templates tailored to various mini-grid scales, ensuring efficiency and consistency in procurement and deployment.
